Dual Gravity Course (DGC)

Heading

DGC is a free course that covers the fundamental concepts of gravitational physics like special relativity, general relativity, and gravitational waves through a supportive and coherent teaching process. This course is a preface for DGR and provides all the knowledge necessary for a researcher in gravitational physics, so participating in DGC prior to DGR is recommended.

What is DGC?

Anyone who trusts his or her mind! DGC is suitable for all knowledge levels, beginning with grade 9 math and physics to students who are proficient in calculus 1 and 2. Also, anybody interested in participating in our free research program (DGR) or in our competition, Graviton, SHOULD attend this course as it provides the necessary knowledge in order to be able to excel in both!

Dual Gravity Research (DGR)

Heading

DGR is a totally FREE research program designed to enable students to unleash their full potential and capabilities in research by using all the possible science integrations to address the peak topics in the field of gravitational physics. The program’s application will be open in August, and will accept less than 100 students internationally, mostly from DGC.

What is DGR?

The research papers that will be authored by DGR students will get resounding recognition in two ways. Firstly, for each paper, a short video that recognizes the author and demonstrates their work will be made and uploaded on our YouTube channel. Secondly, the program will provide publishing opportunities for an unlimited number of researchers according to their papers’ quality.
