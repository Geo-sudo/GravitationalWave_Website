The Propagating Credo

efv

Heading

Spreading modern physics gives real value to our knowledge. We gathered this knowledge, hesitant about its benefits for us as high schoolers, but pure passion pushed us further in the field. It's right that we didn't find a competition or club to invest in our rare passion, but- as the gravitational theory itself- we thought outside the box by creating our investments. Hence the creation of Gravitational Wave- a wave not made by cosmological objects, but instead by the gravitational physics knowledge among students!

Investment of
Knowledge

We believe that scientific knowledge building is a "mountain", and at the top of this mountain is gravitational physics, where the majority of science takes its base. We know that a lot of people love to explore the caves of this mountain, as we do, but we also believe that seeing the magnificent view from the top is a right for every human; it's what the mountain was made for in the first place. Therefore, we always do our best to achieve this right for as many people as we can, and spread the knowledge of gravitational physics!
